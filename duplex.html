Duplex in Luque

Project Details

  • Detail design: Elena Vazquez and Julio Diarte
    • About:

    Year: 2014

    This duplex was designed for a client that wanted to build two houses in his plot in Luque.

    The design of the house is very simple and rational: The social area connects the front and rear pation with large doors that allow for cross ventilation. All the service areas are placed in a single stip aligned towards the midle line of the plot. Upstairs, two bedrooms and a shared bathroom follow the same criteria from the lower level. Large floor to ceiling windows conform the facade of the two houses, with wooden pergolas protecting the entrance of the house.
