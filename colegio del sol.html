Kindergarten School Remodelling

Project Details

  • Detail design and construction supervision: Elena Vazquez and Julio Diarte
    • About:

    Year: 2015, Client: Colegio del sol

    The existing kindergarten occupied a section of a primary and secondary school building that was not initially designed for that purpose. The task was to adapt the existing building to the Reggio Emilia education philosophy focused on experiential learning and relationship driven-methodology. With this in mind, we worked very closely with the director of the school and teachers to redesign the interior spaces, the exterior space, and to introduce a few new components - furniture, stairs, mezzanine - that could increase the interactions and kidâ€™s experiences. The outcome stands out for the openness of the space, the soft natural light, and the convenience of the semi-covered of the new patio.
